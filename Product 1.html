What is Fibr Plus?

Fibr Plus combines the benefits of Fibr broadband and mesh technology into a single service. You can enjoy seamless coverage with no deadspots and experience consistently fast internet speeds throughout your home.

PLDT Fibr Plus plans come with unlimited internet, whole home WiFi TP-Link pucks, and Cignal TV channels. The Fibr Plus Plans’ whole home WiFi system replaces a collection of routers and range extenders with a smart mesh technology that eliminates dead spots anywhere at home for seamless and uninterrupted connectivity.
